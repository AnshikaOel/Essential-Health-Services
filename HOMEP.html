DO'S
    * Wash your hands and use hand sanitiser
    * Don’t touch your face, your eyes, nose, or mouth
    * Do work from home if you can
    * Do use tissues for sneezes and coughs
    * Maintain distance with people who are sick
    * Have good amount of water keep your body hydrated
DON'T
    * Don’t have visitors to your home
    * Don’t hold or attend large gatherings
    * Do not spit on public places
    * Avoid touching railings used by public
    * Do not touch your eyes, mouth or nose without washing hands with water and soap
    * Avoid hugs, kisses and shaking hands while greeting people
